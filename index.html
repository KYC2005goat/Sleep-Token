About Sleep Token

Sleep Token is a collective of anonymous musicians united in their worship of an ancient deity known as ‘Sleep’. Their music transcends genres, blending metal, rock, and R&B with a deeply immersive live experience. Known for their enigmatic stage presence and powerful live performances, Sleep Token creates an atmosphere that transcends the ordinary, inviting listeners into a world where music and ritual collide.

The Music

Sleep Token's discography is a journey through the depths of emotion, combining crushing guitar riffs with ethereal melodies. Their latest album, *This Place Will Become Your Tomb*, explores themes of love, loss, and existential dread, delivered through Vessel's haunting vocals and the band's complex musical arrangements. Each track is an exploration of the human condition, rendered in a soundscape that is both beautiful and terrifying.

Worship

At the heart of Sleep Token's identity is the concept of worship. The band members, who remain masked and nameless, perform in devotion to the deity Sleep. Their live shows are not just concerts but rituals, where music serves as the medium for connection with something beyond the material world. Fans, known as "Worshippers," are drawn into this enigmatic world, where the boundaries between performer and audience blur, creating a collective experience of transcendence.
